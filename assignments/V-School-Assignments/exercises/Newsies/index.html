Winter is Coming!

Winter. Is. Coming.

Take care to winterize your swamp cooler, sprinklers, and hoses. Ensure your hoses are disconnected from your house, so you don't damage the pipes.

Now is the time to purchase winter tires. When the snow is coming down it will be too late!

Also, now is a great time to refresh your emergency kits in your vehicles. That way you can help yourself or someone else in an emergency.

Have a great winter!
